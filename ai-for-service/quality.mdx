Quality AI enables you to:

- Evaluate 100% of customer interactions
- Identify coaching opportunities automatically
- Monitor compliance and adherence
- Drive continuous improvement with data

## How it works

Quality AI analyzes voice, chat, email, and social interactions through speech/text analysis, evaluation scoring, and insight generation. The output flows into three areas:

- **Auto-Scores & Evaluations** — Automated scoring against criteria
- **Coaching Assignments** — Targeted coaching based on evaluation results
- **Compliance Monitoring** — Tracking required disclosures and policy adherence

## Evaluation criteria

### Standard criteria

Pre-built evaluation criteria available out of the box:

| Criteria | Description |
|---|---|
| **Greeting** | Proper introduction and identification |
| **Empathy** | Acknowledging customer emotions |
| **Issue understanding** | Correctly identifying the problem |
| **Resolution** | Providing accurate solution |
| **Closing** | Proper wrap-up and next steps |
| **Compliance** | Following required disclosures |

### Custom criteria

Create custom criteria to match your business needs:

```yaml
Criteria: Product Knowledge
Description: Agent demonstrates accurate product knowledge
Weight: 15%
Scoring:
  - 5: Excellent - Comprehensive, accurate information
  - 4: Good - Mostly accurate with minor gaps
  - 3: Acceptable - Basic knowledge demonstrated
  - 2: Below expectations - Significant gaps
  - 1: Unacceptable - Incorrect information provided
Auto-evaluate: true
```

### Evaluation forms

Group criteria into evaluation forms and apply them to queues:

```yaml
Form: Customer Service Standard
Criteria:
  - greeting (10%)
  - empathy (15%)
  - issue_understanding (20%)
  - resolution (30%)
  - product_knowledge (15%)
  - closing (10%)
Pass threshold: 80%
Apply to:
  - queue: support
  - channel: all
```

## Auto-scoring

AI evaluates interactions in four steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Speech/text analysis">
    Transcribe and analyze the conversation using NLP.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Criteria matching">
    Map conversation content to evaluation criteria.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scoring">
    Assign scores based on evidence found in the conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confidence flagging">
    Flag low-confidence scores for human review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

```yaml
Auto-Scoring:
  enabled: true
  evaluation_rate: 100%  # Evaluate all interactions
  confidence_threshold: 0.8
  human_review:
    - low_confidence_scores
    - failed_evaluations
    - random_sample: 5%
```

## Coaching

### Auto-assignment

Trigger coaching automatically based on evaluation scores:

```yaml
Coaching Rule: Resolution Improvement
Trigger:
  criteria: resolution
  score: < 3
  count: 3 consecutive
Action:
  assign_coaching: resolution_training
  notify: supervisor
  priority: high
```

### Coaching workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Quality issue detected">
    Auto-scoring identifies a pattern requiring coaching.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Coaching assigned">
    Coaching is automatically assigned to the relevant supervisor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Supervisor reviews evidence">
    The supervisor reviews the conversation transcript, recording, and evaluation scorecard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Session scheduled">
    Coaching session is scheduled with the agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow-up evaluation">
    A follow-up evaluation confirms improvement.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Each coaching assignment includes:**

- Conversation transcript
- Audio recording
- Evaluation scorecard
- Specific timestamps and sections
- Comparison to best practices

## Compliance monitoring

### Compliance rules

```yaml
Compliance: PCI-DSS Card Handling
Rules:
  - must_not_say: ["full card number", "CVV"]
  - must_say: ["secure", "encrypted"]
  - action_required: mask_card_data
Alert:
  severity: critical
  notify: compliance_team
```

## Setup Quality AI

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure permissions">
    Go to **User Management > Role Management > New Role > Other Modules**. Assign the Supervisor role or create custom roles with QM permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up Contact Center">
    Assign Supervisors and Auditors to the relevant queues so they can access the right interactions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable features">
    Enable **Conversation Intelligence**, **Auto QA**, and **Bookmarks** in Quality AI **Settings**. Enable **Answer** and **Utterance** suggestions in **GenAI Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create evaluation metrics">
    Choose a measurement type: By Question, Question Answer Pair, or Adherence (Static or Dynamic). Create evaluation metrics and set the count type (Entire Conversation or Time Bound).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create evaluation forms">
    Assign a name, description, channel, and pass score. Select metrics, assign weights, and link the form to queues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze interactions">
    Use Conversation Mining filters to review scored interactions. Save filters to reuse them in audit assignments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create audit allocations">
    Assign interactions to auditors for manual evaluation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run AI-assisted manual audits">
    Use AI-assisted audits for faster, more consistent scoring. Navigate interactions using adherence moments and violations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor performance">
    Use the **Dashboard** to track individual QA progress and queue statistics. Use the **Conversation Intelligence Dashboard** for contact center-wide performance trends.
  </Step>
</Steps>
