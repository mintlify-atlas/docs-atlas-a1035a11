## Release cadence

| Frequency | Details |
|---|---|
| **Standard releases** | Every 15 days with new features, enhancements, and bug fixes |
| **Holiday adjustments** | Schedules may shift around major holidays |
| **Hotfixes** | Critical bug fixes released as needed between standard releases |

## Stay informed

- Subscribe to release notifications from the platform's notification settings.
- Check the release notes for each module to understand what changed and any migration steps required.
- Review the Deprecations section before upgrading to identify any breaking changes.
