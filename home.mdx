## Choose your product

| Product | Best for | Key capabilities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Agent Platform** | Developers building AI-powered applications | Orchestration, tools, RAG, model hub, guardrails |
| **AI for Work** | Enterprise productivity and employee automation | Pre-built agents, custom agents, Teams/Slack integration |
| **AI for Service** | Customer service and contact center operations | Automation AI, Search AI, Contact Center AI, Quality AI |
| **AI for Process** | Knowledge-intensive business process automation | Visual workflow builder, human-in-the-loop, model hub |
